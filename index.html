SIGNATURE TECHNIQUE
THE POWER OF
RAMAN SPECTRA

Every molecule has a unique Raman fingerprint. We decode these spectral signatures to diagnose diseases, detect antibiotic resistance, and monitor metabolic pathways — all without labels or stains.

SYSTEM OVERVIEW
ABOUT IBPL

The central theme of IBPL is to establish and translate an integrated biophotonics approach for imaging and disease diagnosis — from morphology to biochemical dynamic equilibrium.

Our Mission

Utilizing diverse spectroscopic modalities with advanced proteomics and analytical tools to objectively elucidate intrinsic molecular alterations in pathological conditions with high sensitivity and specificity.

Core Techniques

Raman spectroscopy (SERS, DSIP, hyperspectral), fluorescence spectroscopy, SWIR imaging, stable isotope probing, ML-driven bioanalytical pipelines, and confocal Raman imaging with WITec alpha300R.
